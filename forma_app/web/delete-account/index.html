Delete Your GETSA Account

We are sorry to see you go. If you decide to delete your GETSA account, please read the instructions below to understand the process and what happens to your data.

This deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion in one of two ways:

  • In the GETSA Mobile Application:
    Navigate to: Profile → Settings (Gear icon) → Delete Account → Confirm with your password.
  • Request via Email:
    Send an email to nadhalabs@gmail.com from the registered email address associated with your GETSA account, with the subject line "Delete my GETSA account".

What Happens to Your Data?

Once your account deletion request is processed, the following information will be permanently deleted or anonymized:

  • Your account profile (first name, last name, username, and bio)
  • Your username/email association
  • Your uploaded drops, photos, and video media
  • Your comments, reactions (fires/likes), and followers/following data

Data Retention Policy

Please note that some limited records may be retained in our secure backups for a transitional period if required for compliance with legal obligations, platform security, abuse prevention, or backup recovery purposes. Once these retention obligations expire, the data is completely expunged.
